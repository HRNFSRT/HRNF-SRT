About Us

We are a volunteer search and rescue team specializing in emergency response within the Highrock National Forest. Our team is dedicated to ensuring the safety of all visitors and residents, offering a wide range of rescue and emergency services.

Recent Rescues

Mountain Rescue - October 2024

A successful rescue of two hikers lost near Liberty Peak. The operation lasted 12 hours in challenging conditions.

Flooded River Rescue - September 2024

Swift-water rescue to save a stranded kayaker during unexpected flooding. The team responded within 20 minutes.
