Highrock National Forest SRT - Who We Are:

The Highrock National Forest Search and Rescue (HRNF S&R) Company is a specialized unit dedicated to providing high-quality search and rescue services within the expansive and challenging terrain of the Highrock National Forest. Our mission is to protect lives through rapid, effective response to emergencies, including wilderness rescues, technical rescues, and disaster response.

Recent Rescues

October 10th, Riverside Drive, Major Incident involving a boating accident

HRNF SRT Deployed to a Major Incident along Riverside. Upon arrival The team was informed that a MOB (man overboard) had sunk below and could not be found. Following this the Dive Team was called and the MOB was successfully rescued. The person made a full recovery due to the swift action of the HRNF SRT Team.

Our Company Values

Safety: The safety of our team members and the individuals we rescue is our top priority. We adhere to strict protocols and continuously train to ensure that every operation is conducted with the utmost care.

Professionalism: We operate with integrity, accountability, and respect. Every team member is expected to maintain the highest standards of conduct, both on and off the field.

Excellence: We pursue excellence in every aspect of our work, from planning and training to execution and debriefing. Our commitment to quality drives us to constantly improve our skills and procedures.

Teamwork: Collaboration is essential to our success. We work closely with each other and with partner agencies, fostering a culture of mutual support and effective communication.
